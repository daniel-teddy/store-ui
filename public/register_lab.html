Terms and conditioins

Eligibility:

  • Anyone is welcome to become a student. There are no specific eligibility criteria for enrollment.

Class Grouping:

  • Classes are categorized into groups based on age and the student's knowledge level of the course they wish to undertake. This ensures an optimal learning experience for all students.

Class Schedule:

  • Classes are conducted twice a week, with each session lasting one hour.
  • A detailed timetable and course outline will be provided upon registration.

Payment Policy:

  • Tuition fees of 4000 TL ($150) must be paid at the beginning of each month.
  • Payment of the monthly fee is non refundable.
  • Payment options and due dates will be communicated separately.

Course Duration:

  • The total duration of the course is 2 months.

Equipment Requirement:

  • Students are required to bring their own laptops to class.
  • Having a laptop is essential for accessing course materials and participating in class activities.

Code of Conduct:

  • All students are expected to adhere to the code of conduct, which includes but is not limited to the following:
  • Behaving respectfully towards instructors and fellow students.
  • Maintaining a positive and inclusive learning environment.
  • Respecting the property and equipment of the institution and fellow students, which includes not breaking or damaging anything.
  • Avoiding loitering in the institution's premises.
  • Any violation of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary actions, including suspension or expulsion.

Course Content:

  • The course will include assignments, class exercises, and projects to enhance the learning experience.

Attendance:

  • Regular attendance is important for a successful learning experience.
  • If you are unable to attend a class, please inform your instructor in advance.

Privacy Policy:

  • Your personal information will be handled in accordance with our privacy policy, which is available for review upon request.

Changes to Terms and Conditions:

  • The institution reserves the right to update or modify these terms and conditions as necessary. Students will be informed of any changes.
